  • Ground-Water-Level-Prediction

    View on Github

    Python,seaborn, scipy numPy, Pandas

    At first, we preprocess the data, then used many types of regression models to predict the ground level water in different regions of Bangladesh to install deep tube wells. For seasonal forecasting, we are using ARIMA, LSTM and GRU models. It is a funded research project by WARPO (Water Resources Planning Organization) under the Ministry of Water Resources, Government of Bangladesh. Supervisor: Dr. A. B. M. Alim Al Islam (Professor, CSE, BUET)

  • Vehicle Object Detection in context of BD traffic

    View on Github

    Pytorch, Huggingface, Sklearn

    We address complex task of object detection in driving environments across 9 districts in Bangladesh using BadODD dataset. This dataset contains 9825 images with 78,943 Objects with 13 different classes. We employed transfer learning using three different approaches, such as : YOLOv6 and YOLOv8, Novel transformer-based models such as rtDETR and CoDETR. We also explored Faster R-CNN. It was a part of SUST Deep Learning Enigma competition in the field of Computer Vision, where we secured 8th position among around 100 teams.
